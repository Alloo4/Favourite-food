Easy Rice and Chicken Recipe

A simple and delicious recipe for classic rice and chicken.

Overview

Rice and chicken is a comforting, hearty meal that's perfect for any night of the week. This dish is easy to make, full of flavor, and always satisfies hunger.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • (Optional) Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 5-7 minutes per side. ü•ò
  2. Remove chicken from the skillet and set aside. ü•Ñ
  3. Add chopped onion to the skillet and cook until softened, about 3 minutes. üç≥
  4. Add garlic and dried oregano to the pan, cook for an additional minute until fragrant. üåø
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Stir in the rice, salt, and pepper. üç≤
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through and the liquid is absorbed.
  7. Shred or slice the cooked chicken breasts and return them to the pan with the rice.
  8. (Optional) Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.

Cooking Tips

  • For extra flavor, marinate the chicken in your favorite spices for at least an hour before cooking.
  • Use fresh herbs like thyme or rosemary instead of dried oregano for a different taste.
  • Try adding vegetables like peas, carrots, or bell peppers to the rice for added nutrition and color.

Notes

When the dish is done, it should look golden and slightly crispy with a vibrant green parsley garnish. The aroma will be a tantalizing blend of garlic and oregano, with a rich undertone of chicken broth. The taste should be a harmonious balance of savory, slightly salty flavors with a hint of earthy herbs. The chicken will be tender and juicy, perfectly complemented by the fluffy, flavorful rice.

Enjoy your delicious and easy rice and chicken!
